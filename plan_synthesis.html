RXN for Chemistry - Plan a Synthesis

This guide is a short tutorial to quickly learn how to use RXN for Chemistry to plan and execute a synthesis starting from several different initial starting points.

Start Tutorial
An account on a SaaS instance of RXN for Chemistry is required to complete this tutorial.

  • Estimated time to complete this tutorial: nn minutes

Step 2: From the Home screen click "Plan a synthesis"

Step 3: Select "Add to pre-existing project" or "Add to a brand new project"
